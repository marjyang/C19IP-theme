Are we still paying attention?

COVID-19 exposed systemic racial inequities, disproportionately impacting communities of color. How can we repair the breach and build resilience for our communities?

Our goal is to make the COVID-19 Impact Project the leading data storyteller of the COVID-19 pandemic by documenting the pandemic’s unequal impact on communities of color, and sharing the historical precedents and lingering effects of this ongoing inequity.

EXTRACTING STORIES FROM DATA

In the Fall of 2020 we were invited by Dan O’Sullivan, Associate Dean for Emerging Media at New York University’s Tisch School of the Arts, to create a course for the graduate students at ITP. This course would broaden the exploration of the unfolding pandemic data and invite graduate students to contribute to the COVID-19 Impact Project according to their skills and interests in emerging media.
